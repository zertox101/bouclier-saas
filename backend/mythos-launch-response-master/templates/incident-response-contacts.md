## First 60 Minutes Checklist

If you suspect a breach, do these in order:

1. [ ] **Don't panic.** Follow this sheet.
2. [ ] **Call the Incident Commander** (top of this page)
3. [ ] **Do NOT turn off compromised systems** (preserves forensic evidence) — unless actively spreading (ransomware)
4. [ ] **Disconnect compromised systems from the network** (unplug ethernet, disable Wi-Fi)
5. [ ] **Change passwords** for any accounts that may be compromised — from a DIFFERENT device
6. [ ] **Call your cyber insurance carrier** — they often have an IR firm on retainer for you
7. [ ] **Document everything** with timestamps: what you observed, when, what actions you took
8. [ ] **Do NOT communicate about the incident via potentially compromised systems** — use personal phones if needed
9. [ ] **Do NOT pay ransom** without consulting legal counsel and your insurance carrier first
10. [ ] **Preserve evidence** — don't delete logs, don't wipe systems, don't "clean up"
