# The Mythos Threat: What Every Business Needs to Know Right Now

*If you read nothing else in this repo, read this page.*

---

## What Happened

On April 7, 2026, an AI system proved it can find and exploit security holes in every major operating system and web browser — holes that human experts missed for up to 27 years. The cost: as little as **$50 per vulnerability**. Previously, this required nation-state intelligence agencies with million-dollar budgets.

## Why It Matters to You

- **AI makes attacking your business nearly free.** Hackers no longer need elite skills.
- **Attacks can be developed within hours** of a vulnerability being discovered. You no longer have weeks to install updates.
- **Small businesses are not protected** by the emergency coalition (Project Glasswing) that Anthropic formed with Apple, Microsoft, Google, and others.
- **No government agency has issued guidance for small businesses** as of April 11, 2026.

## What to Do This Week

1. **Turn on automatic updates** on every computer, phone, and tablet. Right now.
2. **Enable multi-factor authentication** on your email, banking, and cloud accounts. Start with email — it's the master key.
3. **Get a password manager** (Bitwarden is free). Stop reusing passwords.
4. **Back up your data** to a drive you physically disconnect from your network.
5. **Ask yourself:** Is anything internet-facing that doesn't need to be? If yes, take it offline.

## What to Do This Month

6. **Get cyber insurance** if you don't have it — premiums are expected to spike.
7. **Replace your antivirus** with Endpoint Detection and Response (EDR) — traditional antivirus can't catch new AI-discovered threats.
8. **Write down your incident response plan** — who do you call if you get hacked? Know before it happens.
9. **Separate your guest Wi-Fi** from your business network.
10. **Train your staff:** The new rule is "verify everything sensitive through a second channel." AI makes phishing emails perfect.
